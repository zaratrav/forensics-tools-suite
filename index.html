About Forensics Tools

Digital forensics tools are used to collect, analyze, and interpret evidence from digital systems.

Autopsy and Sleuth Kit provide open-source forensic analysis capabilities.

FTK and EnCase are industry-standard tools used in professional investigations.

SANS SIFT and REMnux offer specialized environments for DFIR and malware analysis.

Tools like ExifTool, Binwalk, and strings enable low-level data inspection and extraction.

Choosing the right tool depends on the investigation type, data source, and required depth of analysis.
